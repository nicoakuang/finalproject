About The Project

This project introduces "Adagio," a system designed to solve the common problem of hearing a song but not knowing its name. Using audio fingerprinting technology, our web-based platform allows users to quickly identify music from various sources. We integrated the AudD and AcrCloud APIs for robust recognition, enhanced with real-time lyrics from Genius and lyrics.ovh. A key innovation is our generative AI-powered search engine, which lets users find songs using natural language, descriptions, or moods.

Our Signature Feature: In-Device Recognition

This is the game-changing tool that sets Adagio apart. Ever heard a song in a Netflix show, a YouTube video, or a game on your computer and desperately wanted to know what it was?

With Adagio, you no longer need to grab your phone and struggle to record the audio. Simply play the media on your device, open our website, and start recognition. Adagio listens directly to your computer's audio output and identifies the song for you‚Äîseamlessly and instantly.

More Key Features

Dual-Engine Recognition

We use both AudD and AcrCloud APIs to maximize recognition accuracy across English, Mandarin, and other languages.

AI-Powered Search

Integrated with ChatGPT, users can search for music with descriptive text like "a happy Taiwanese song" or "a relaxing English melody."

Multi-Source Input

Recognize music by uploading an audio file, capturing browser audio, or using your device's microphone.

Rich Result Display

Successful recognition provides song title, artist, album, real-time lyrics, and direct links to YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Music.
